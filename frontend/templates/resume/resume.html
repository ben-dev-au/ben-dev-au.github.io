My Journey

From sommelier to small business manager to software engineer...

Scotland Early Years — 2009

Sommelier Career Begins

Entered the wine & fine dining industry, beginning a journey through cellars, vineyards, and fine dining rooms across the globe.

Montalcino, Italy 2010 — 2012

Head Sommelier & Fine Dining Awards

Formative period as Group Head Sommelier for one of Australia's top regional fine dining restaurants, gaining experience working in local wineries by day, and working the restaurants by night.

Royal Mail Hotel 2012 — 2015

New States, New benchmarks

Sommelier roles in Vic and WA — earning top industry accolades as part of world class teams: 2x Australian Restaurant of the Year, 3x Australian Wine List of the Year, 2x Wine Spectator's Grand Award (2 of 3 awarded in Au)...

The Cellar 2015 — 2018

Winemaking Studies

Studied Viticulture & Oenology at the University of Adelaide — deepening technical knowledge of winemaking from vine to bottle through hands-on cellar work.

Independent Wine Retail 2018 — 2023

Business Leadership & Overhaul

Joined as a casual while studying and finished as General Manager; led a complete business and culture overhaul — from bookkeeping to building an e-commerce platform, modernised operations, and returned the business to profitability.

Adelaide University 2024 — 2027

Career Pivot & University

Transitioned from leveraging technology within wine to building technology as a core profession. Enrolled in Bachelor of Software Engineering (Honours) — formalising & building upon the skills developed through years of self-directed problem solving.

Building 2025 — Present

Software Engineer & Continuing Student

Studying Software Engineering at Adelaide University while building full-stack applications with Python, FastAPI, React, and TypeScript.
